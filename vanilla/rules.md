# Vanilla SMP Rules

These rules apply **only to the Vanilla SMP**. They do not replace the Buffed Network Rules – they add on top of them. Every player on the Vanilla SMP is bound by both documents at all times.

> Before reading this, make sure you have read and understood the **Buffed Network Rules**. Network Rules always take priority. Nothing written here can override them.

The Vanilla SMP is a public Vanilla server with small adjustments, the game stays Vanilla at its core, with quality-of-life and fun features added on top to make the experience more accessible and enjoyable without distorting the Vanilla feel. There is no buff system, no essences, no rituals – the focus is on fair, vanilla-style gameplay with a balanced PvP environment where no single combat style dominates.


---

## A. COMBAT RULES

- You are in combat whenever an enemy is visible to you, and for **3 minutes** after your last hit dealt or taken.
- No combat logging. No danger logging.
- No Elytra in combat or while in a danger zone.
- No Riptide trident in fights.
- **Crystal PvP is forbidden.** End Crystals + Anchors may not be used as a weapon.
- No bed bombing. Using beds to deal explosion damage.
- No abusing mechanics in fights – including but not limited to excessive ender pearl spam.
- No camping or harming AFK players.
- No water-running or lava-running mechanics.
- No `/gsit` or similar sit-plugin abuse during combat.
- After dying or fleeing a fight, you may only re-engage after **10 minutes**.
- If a player or staff tells you to **"land"** (or similar), you must land immediately. This applies to anything you can fly or stay airborne with – Elytra, Riptide, Happy Ghasts and similar. Refusal is treated as elytra-in-combat.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Vanilla SMP staff may take action against any combat behavior deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*

---

## B. ITEMS & LIMITS

- **Slow Falling is banned** in combat and danger zones – no Slow Falling potions or arrows.
- **Weakness is banned** in combat and danger zones – no Weakness potions or arrows.
- Max 8 Totems per fight
- Health indicators are not allowed, except through our own mod: [Health Indicators Fairplay](https://modrinth.com/mod/health-indicators-fairplay).
- No item duplication of any kind.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Vanilla SMP staff may take action against any item-related behavior deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*

---

## C. WORLD & BASES

- Bases must be visible and reachable. No fully hidden or inaccessible bases.
- Bases must be within **8,000 blocks of spawn**.
- No stasis chamber use in combat.
- No griefing of other players' builds. This also includes killing villagers, or removing items from farms in a way that breaks them. Orbital Strikes may not be used on bases.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Vanilla SMP staff may take action against any world-related behavior deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*

---

## D. FAIR PLAY & PvP CONDUCT

- No unfair PvP mechanics or mechanic abuse.
- No constant running from fights to avoid death – sustained avoidance counts as combat evasion.
- No kill farming. No killing yourself in combat.
- No abusing the LootDrop or Orbital Strike systems to harm, grief, or harass other players outside legitimate gameplay.
- No behavior intended to ruin fun or pressure players into quitting. Rivalry is part of the game; targeted harassment campaigns are not.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Vanilla SMP staff may take action against any conduct deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*

---

## E. BOUNTIES & ECONOMY

- No abuse, farming, or private agreements involving bounties.
- Bounties may only be claimed through legitimate PvP – no staged kills, arranged deaths, or deals.
- The Sparks economy is meant to be earned through real gameplay. Any scheme to generate or transfer Sparks outside of intended mechanics is forbidden – including staged kills or arranged deaths to game kill rewards or streaks.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Vanilla SMP staff may take action against any behavior involving bounties or the economy deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*
