# BuffSMP Rules

These rules apply **only to BuffSMP**. They do not replace the Buffed Network Rules — they add on top of them. Every player on BuffSMP is bound by both documents at all times.

> Before reading this, make sure you have read and understood the **Buffed Network Rules**. Network Rules always take priority. Nothing written here can override them.

---

## A. COMBAT RULES

- No combat logging. No danger logging.
- No Elytra in combat or while in a danger zone.
- No Riptide trident in fights.
- No explosives in PvP: Crystals, Anchors, Beds, Minecarts. **TNT traps are allowed** when used as base defense — not as a combat weapon.
- No abusing mechanics in fights — including but not limited to excessive ender pearl spam.
- No camping or harming AFK players.
- No water-running or lava-running mechanics.
- No `/gsit` or similar sit-plugin abuse during combat.
- After dying or fleeing a fight, you may only re-engage after **20 minutes**.
- If a player or staff says **"land"**, you must land immediately. Refusal is treated as elytra-in-combat.

*These rules are not exhaustive. BuffSMP staff may take action against any combat behavior deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*

---

## B. ITEMS & LIMITS

- No debuff potions, including Invisibility.
- No debuff or tipped arrows.
- Maximum **6 totems** per fight.
- Maximum **3 stacks of XP bottles** per fight.
- Maces are forbidden.
- No repairing armor with anything other than XP bottles. No mending exploit, no anvil-cycling tricks.
- **Server kit balance applies.** Players must follow the intended kit. Off-kit combinations (e.g., Netherite armor + Shield) are not allowed unless a staff member has explicitly granted an exception in writing.

*These rules are not exhaustive. BuffSMP staff may take action against any item-related behavior deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*

---

## C. WORLD & BASES

- Bases must be visible and reachable. No fully hidden or inaccessible bases.
- Bases must be within **6,000 blocks of spawn**.
- No stasis chamber abuse.
- No griefing.
- No excessive or unnecessary stealing. Targeted theft as part of legitimate rivalry is acceptable — emptying a casual player's chests for no reason is not.

*These rules are not exhaustive. BuffSMP staff may take action against any world-related behavior deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*

---

## D. TEAMING & PvP CONDUCT

- No cross-teaming with other groups during fights or events.
- No mega-teams. Ideal team size is **3 players**. Staff may intervene if a larger grouping persists and affects server balance.
- No unfair PvP mechanics (mechanic abuse, kit-stuffing, off-kit gear).
- No constant running from abilities or fights to avoid death — sustained avoidance counts as combat evasion.
- No kill farming. No killing yourself in combat.
- No behavior intended to ruin fun or pressure players into quitting. Rivalry is part of the game; targeted harassment campaigns are not.

*These rules are not exhaustive. BuffSMP staff may take action against any conduct deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*

---

## E. BOUNTIES & UNIQUE ITEMS

### Bounties

- No abuse, farming, or private agreements involving bounties.
- Bounties may only be claimed through legitimate PvP — no staged kills, arranged deaths, or deals.

### Unique / Special Items

- Unique items must always be in the inventory of an **active player**.
- The holder must be regularly active, especially during peak hours.
- Extended inactivity may result in the item being transferred or removed by staff.
- Avoiding fights, hiding the item, or refusing to take risks in order to protect it creates an unfair advantage and is not allowed.
- No private agreements, safekeeping deals, or non-aggression pacts built around unique items.

*These rules are not exhaustive. BuffSMP staff may take action against any behavior involving bounties or unique items deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the server, even if not explicitly listed.*
