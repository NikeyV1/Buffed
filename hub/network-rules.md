# Buffed Network Rules

These rules apply across the **entire Buffed network** — every Buffed SMP, the Discord server, and any other official Buffed platform, at all times. Every server under the Buffed name has its own additional rules on top of these — but nothing in those rules can override or weaken what is written here.

> **Use common sense. Do not try to loophole or bend the rules.**
> If something feels wrong, it probably is. Not every situation can be listed — staff decisions cover the spirit, not just the letter.

---

## 1. CHAT & COMMUNICATION

These rules apply to every form of communication on Buffed: in-game chat, Discord, signs, books, item names, voice channels, and DMs between members.

- No racism, bigotry, sexism, homophobia, or other discriminatory speech.
- No harassment, targeting, or disrespect toward players or staff.
- No NSFW content (chat, signs, books, builds, skins, capes, item names).
- No statements encouraging suicide, self-harm, or wishing serious harm on others. Jokes or casual references count and may be punished depending on severity.
- No spam, flooding, or excessive caps in public chat.
- No drama escalation in public channels — take it to a ticket.
- No threats of any kind, including DDoS, doxxing, swatting, or SWAT-style threats. "Just joking" is not a defense.
- No advertising other servers, products, communities, or services — including via DM, VC, or hidden links.
- No bypassing the chat filter (creative spelling, Unicode tricks, etc. all count as bypass).
- No real-world item trading or selling of in-game items, accounts, or services for real money.
- No sharing or linking content that violates someone's privacy.
- No false or harmful claims about Buffed staff or members without clear evidence.
- Public chat: **English or German only.**
- No disrespectful remarks about deceased individuals.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Buffed staff may take action against any communication deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the network, even if not explicitly listed.*

## 2. PLATFORM-WIDE RULES

These rules apply to your conduct as a Buffed member across every channel — game, Discord, tickets, appeals.

- Follow staff instructions at all times. You can dispute decisions through tickets, not by ignoring them.
- Do not lie to staff, in tickets, or in screenshares.
- Do not falsify, alter, or fabricate evidence against another player.
- Do not impersonate staff members or other players (names, skins, capes, Discord profiles).
- Do not encourage, pressure, or coerce others into breaking rules.
- Do not blackmail or extort players or staff.
- Do not evade punishments (alts, VPNs, account swaps, asking others to act on your behalf).
- Do not knowingly associate with banned or blacklisted players in ways that help them evade punishment. Continued association may itself result in action.
- Do not reopen tickets, appeals, or reports that have already been resolved.
- Do not abuse staff reporting systems — this includes tickets, report channels, staff DMs, or any other official channel available on a given server. These systems exist for legitimate issues only.
- Private conversations — including those sent directly between players through in-game means — carry no weight as network evidence or binding agreements unless they were explicitly established and confirmed on the network itself (e.g., through an official ticket or staff decision). Exceptions may apply at staff discretion.
- Report rule violations you witness. Ignoring or covering for rule-breakers may count as participation.
- Do not interfere with or obstruct staff investigations.
- Inactivity warning: Extended AFK or inactivity may result in a kick. Buffed reserves the right to free up slots, items, or roles if a holder becomes long-term inactive.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Buffed staff may take action against any conduct deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the network, even if not explicitly listed.*

## 3. FAIR PLAY & INTEGRITY

These rules cover gameplay integrity across every Buffed server.

- No hacking, cheating, or any client modification that gives an unfair advantage. Anything not explicitly allowed in the modification list is forbidden — claiming uncertainty is not a defense.
- No X-Ray of any kind (mods, texture packs, ore highlighters, transparency tricks, exposed-cave packs).
- No duping or bug abuse. Found a bug? Report it in a ticket — abusing it is punishable, reporting it can be rewarded.
- No exploiting unintended game mechanics for advantage.
- No seed cracking or world-data extraction.
- No causing intentional server lag, mass-bot connections, or anything that harms server performance.
- No alt-accounts. One account per player. Exceptions only with prior written staff approval.
- No account sharing. If your account is used by someone else — for any reason — every action is treated as yours and you may be banned. Compromised accounts will not have progress restored.
- No stream sniping (using a streamer's broadcast to gain combat or location info).
- No playtime farming. Standing AFK while actively doing something (e.g., at a farm) is fine. Parking yourself somewhere for extended periods without any purpose — purely to accumulate playtime, rewards, or to avoid being marked inactive — is not allowed. Anti-AFK pools, weighted keys, macros, and autoclickers are never allowed.
- Your Minecraft username and skin must not be objectionable. Staff may require a change.
- No racist, NSFW, or offensive builds, signs, item names, or pixel art.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Buffed staff may take action against any gameplay behavior deemed harmful, disruptive, or against the spirit of the network, even if not explicitly listed.*

## 4. MODIFICATIONS & CLIENTS

Mods are used at your own risk. **If a mod gives any unfair advantage, it is not allowed — even if it's not on the blacklist below.** Common sense always applies.

### Allowed Modifications

The following are explicitly allowed on the Buffed network:

**Performance & visual clarity**
- Sodium, Lithium, Starlight, Iris, FerriteCore, Krypton
- OptiFine
- Sodium-style shaders that don't reveal hidden information
- Zoomify, OptiFine zoom
- Fullbright / Gamma Utils
- Fog removal texture packs or mods — **only** for natural fog (lava, water, atmosphere). Effects caused by status effects such as Blindness or Darkness must remain fully visible.

**HUD mods (player info)**
- FPS, CPS, Coordinates, Direction, Saturation, Armor durability, Potion HUD, Item Counter, Direction HUD
- Totem Counter (your own totems or nearby players)

**Quality-of-life**
- AppleSkin
- Shulker Box Tooltip
- Essential
- Litematica (**building mode only** — Printer and any auto-placement features are not allowed)
- **Replay Mod** — allowed under strict conditions: recording only, no live use during or around combat. Staff may individually prohibit a player from using Replay Mod at any time if there is reason to believe it is being misused (e.g., scouting underground, viewing through walls). When in doubt, ask staff before using it.

**Cosmetic**
- Capes, custom crosshairs, custom skins, cosmetic shaders, sound mods
- Texture packs that do not provide a competitive or visual advantage

### Disallowed Modifications

The following are not allowed under any circumstances:

**Cheating / advantage clients**
- X-Ray (any form, including texture packs)
- Reach / Hitbox modifiers
- Aim Assist, Killaura, Triggerbot
- ESP, Player Tracers, Player Radar
- Item Indicators
- Loot Beams

**Automation**
- Auto-clickers, auto-totem, auto-anchor, auto-crystal, auto-miners
- Litematica Printer / Schematica Printer / any auto-builder
- AutoFish, AutoSorter macros, AutoEat
- Macros that bind multiple actions to one key beyond standard mouse use

**Movement & timing**
- Speed mods, Timer mods, Step / Spider, Flight, Free Move, Freecam
- Wall-Jump, Ladder Warp, Faster Ladder Climbing, Jump Over Fences
- No-Jump-Delay, No-Input-Lag, anything that modifies tick rate

**Inventory & placement**
- Inventory Move, Inventory Profiles Next, Mouse Tweaks, Item Scroller
- Accurate Block Placement, Pro Placer, Smart Block Placement, Cut Through
- Tweakaroo
- Schematic mods of any kind beyond plain Litematica building mode

**Connection & ping**
- VPNs, ExitLag, or any connection-routing service. Exceptions are not guaranteed — open a ticket first.
- Clumsy or any tool that simulates, manipulates, or spoofs ping
- ViaFabric, ViaForge, Multiconnect, or any client allowing unsupported version connections

**Other**
- Click Crystals, Crystal Optimizer (auto-targeting variants)
- Anchor Optimizer (auto-targeting variants)
- Client Commands
- Drag-clicking is permitted at your own risk — if it crosses into automation territory, it will still be punished.

*This list is not exhaustive. Any mod, resource pack, or client modification that provides an advantage outside of vanilla gameplay is forbidden by default. If you are unsure, ask in a ticket before using it.*

## 5. SCREENSHARING RULES

When staff suspects a violation, they may freeze you and request a screenshare. Refusal or interference is treated the same as being caught cheating.

- Refusing to screenshare = ban.
- Closing Minecraft, killing the process, or disconnecting while frozen = immediate ban.
- You must join the Discord screenshare voice channel as quickly as possible after being requested. Delaying, stalling, or intentionally wasting time may result in punishment. Failure to join within 3 minutes may be treated as refusal to screenshare.
- Custom or modified operating systems are not encouraged. Using one is at your own risk — if essential services are missing, that may also result in a ban.

The following actions are forbidden during the session in which you are frozen, and immediately before a screenshare begins:

- Clearing Event Logs
- Clearing the USN Journal or Prefetch
- Modifying registry values
- Running system cleaners (CCleaner, etc.)
- Modifying or clearing the Recycle Bin
- Restarting Explorer, Minecraft, or other relevant applications
- Restarting important Windows services after boot
- Modifying, deleting, renaming, or replacing relevant files
- Executing any cheat or modification during the current session
- Running coding or scripting environments during the current session
- Using anti-scanner or evasion programs
- Running VeraCrypt, BitLocker, or similar disk-encryption boot tools

The following Windows services must be enabled. Disabling them interferes with screensharing and results in a ban:

- SysMain (Prefetch / Superfetch)
- DPS (Diagnostic Policy Service)
- PcaSvc (Program Compatibility Assistant)
- EventLog
- BAM (Background Activity Moderator)
- DComLaunch
- WSearch (Windows Search)
- Scheduler (Task Scheduler)
- Registry Logging

If services are disabled, you may be asked to re-enable them and reboot before the screenshare continues.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Staff may request additional checks if behavior, history, or evidence warrants it.*

- All Patreon / store purchases are final.

- Roles, perks, or unique items granted by staff or Patreon may be revoked if used to abuse the network or its rules.

*These rules are not exhaustive. Buffed staff may take action against any account-related behavior deemed abusive, even if not explicitly listed.*
